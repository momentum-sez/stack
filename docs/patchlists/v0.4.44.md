# GENESIS v0.4.44 Patchlist

## Release Summary

Version 0.4.44 GENESIS transforms the MSEZ Stack from infrastructure into a fully deployable Special Economic Zone. This release completes the SEZ-in-a-Box vision with three new module families, the Pack Trilogy (lawpacks + regpacks + licensepacks), multi-jurisdiction composition, and one-click AWS deployment.

**Codename:** GENESIS — The beginning of operational SEZ deployment.

## New Module Families

### Corporate Services (8 modules)
Complete Corporate Service Provider (CSP) infrastructure:
- `corporate/formation` — Incorporation workflows, articles, memorandum, bylaws
- `corporate/registered-agent` — Registered office and agent services
- `corporate/secretarial` — Board minutes, resolutions, annual returns
- `corporate/beneficial-ownership` — UBO registry with verification chain
- `corporate/governance-templates` — Articles of association, SHA templates
- `corporate/annual-compliance` — Filing calendars, deadlines, auto-reminders
- `corporate/dissolution` — Voluntary/involuntary wind-down procedures
- `corporate/cap-table` — Share capital, equity, vesting schedules

### Identity & Credentialing (4 modules)
Verified identity from pseudonymous to institutional:
- `identity/core` — Decentralized identifiers with key management
- `identity/kyc-tiers` — Progressive KYC Tier 0-3 verification workflows
- `identity/credentials` — Professional licenses and certifications
- `identity/binding` — Entity-to-identity-to-instrument linkage

### Tax & Revenue (4 modules)
Fiscal framework and incentive structure:
- `tax/framework` — Zone tax regime (rates, exemptions, incentives)
- `tax/fee-schedules` — Zone operating fees, license fees, filing fees
- `tax/reporting` — Tax compliance reporting automation
- `tax/incentive-programs` — Investment credits, holidays, reduced rates

### Capital Markets (8 modules)
Securities issuance through post-trade settlement:
- `capital-markets/securities-issuance` — Primary market issuance workflows
- `capital-markets/trading` — Order book and matching engine specification
- `capital-markets/post-trade` — Trade confirmation, allocation, settlement
- `capital-markets/csd` — Central Securities Depository
- `capital-markets/clearing` — Central Counterparty clearing
- `capital-markets/dvp-pvp` — Delivery vs Payment, Payment vs Payment
- `capital-markets/surveillance` — Real-time market monitoring and alerts
- `capital-markets/corporate-actions` — Dividends, splits, mergers, rights

### Trade & Commerce (5 modules)
International trade finance infrastructure:
- `trade/letters-of-credit` — Documentary and standby LC workflows
- `trade/trade-documents` — Bills of lading, invoices, certificates
- `trade/supply-chain-finance` — Reverse factoring, dynamic discounting
- `trade/customs` — Tariffs and customs procedures
- `trade/trade-insurance` — Trade credit and cargo insurance

## New Infrastructure

### Licensepack Specification (`spec/98-licensepacks.md`)
Completing the Pack Trilogy (lawpacks + regpacks + licensepacks):
- Real-time license status tracking with registry snapshots
- Expiry monitoring with configurable refresh policies
- Compliance attestation workflow integration
- Content-addressed license artifacts

### Multi-Jurisdiction Composition Engine (`tools/msez/composition.py`)
Enables complex zone deployments combining laws from multiple jurisdictions:
```python
zone = compose_zone(
    "hybrid.demo",
    "NYC-Delaware-ADGM Hybrid Zone",
    civic=("us-ny", "New York civic code"),
    corporate=("us-de", "Delaware corporate law"),
    financial=("ae-abudhabi-adgm", "ADGM financial services"),
    ai_arbitration=True,
)
```

### AWS Deployment Automation
Production-ready Terraform modules:
- `deploy/aws/terraform/main.tf` — Complete AWS infrastructure (VPC, EKS, RDS, ElastiCache, S3, KMS)
- `deploy/aws/terraform/kubernetes.tf` — Kubernetes deployments with IRSA
- `deploy/aws/terraform/examples/hybrid-zone.tfvars` — Example configuration

### Docker Container Images
- `Dockerfile.corridor-node` — Corridor state machine node
- `Dockerfile.zone-authority` — Zone authority services
- `Dockerfile.watcher` — Watcher attestation node
